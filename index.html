About the Project

CVL-CDSAML is a comprehensive research initiative funded by the Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (HFRI/ELIDEK) that develops an open-access corpus-based valency lexicon for ancient and medieval languages. The project combines traditional philological methods with cutting-edge computational linguistics and AI to analyze verb argument structures across time and languages.

Research Objectives

Valency Patterns

Systematic extraction and analysis of verb valency patterns from diachronic corpora

Cross-linguistic Analysis

Comparative study of argument structure across Indo-European languages

Diachronic Change

Tracking syntactic evolution from antiquity to modern times

AI-Powered Tools

Development of computational methods for automated linguistic analysis

Open Science

All data, tools, and findings freely available under CC-BY license

Interactive Platform

Web-based interface for researchers worldwide to query and analyze data
